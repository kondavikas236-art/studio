# **App Name**: Mindful Play

## Core Features:

- Kid's Usage Dashboard: A gamified, visually appealing dashboard for children to track their daily screen time across app categories with a 'Focus Meter' or 'Energy Bar' visual metaphor.
- Parental Settings & Insights: A secure section for parents to view detailed screen time reports and set overall daily time limits for their child's device usage.
- AI-Powered Smart Break Prompts: A generative AI tool that analyzes usage patterns to provide gentle, context-aware prompts for eye rests and suggestions for alternative activities to encourage healthy breaks.
- Guided Eye Health Activities: Short, engaging mini-games (e.g., Focus Maze, Blink Buddy) designed to reduce eye strain and improve visual focus.
- AI Story Chain Companion: An AI tool that initiates story prompts and collaboratively guides children to add sentences, fostering creative writing and imagination as a "Brain Fog Buster".
- Positive Reinforcement System: A reward system that encourages healthy habits through badges, virtual rewards, or avatar customization for completing activities and taking breaks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calm, thoughtful blue (#2E8AB8). This hue evokes mental clarity and trust, supporting the app's 'mindful' and health-focused core.
- Background color: A very light, subtly blue-tinted gray (#F0F3F5). This provides a clean, open, and gentle canvas, creating a welcoming and non-aggressive environment.
- Accent color: A vibrant, lively lime green/yellow-green (#CFE467). Used for calls to action, achievements, and positive feedback elements to symbolize growth and reward.
- All text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly legible design, ensuring ease of reading for both children and parents across all app sections.
- Custom icons and illustrations should be friendly, rounded, and non-judgmental. Incorporate charming, character-based avatars and elements that support positive reinforcement.
- For children, prioritize clean, card-based layouts with ample white space and large, touch-friendly interactive elements. The parental dashboard should feature clear hierarchy and robust controls.
- Implement subtle, playful animations for transitions, positive feedback, and rewarding actions (e.g., when a goal is met or an activity is completed) to enhance engagement and motivation.
